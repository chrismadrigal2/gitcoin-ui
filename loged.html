What is Gitcoin?
Gitcoin is a way for people to easily give one-time or weekly donations to their favorite projects. Think of it as social philanthropy using bitcoin. We are currently built on top of the Coinbase API which means that in order to receive donation with Gitcoin you'll need to have a Coinbase account. Why Coinbase?
We think Coinbase rocks! They are the most legit place to buy and sell bitcoin and they are also the place that we ourselves store our online wallets.Coinbase is located in San Francisco and has raised money from the best VCs out there. Also, there are more practical reasons for using Coinbase. They offer offblockchain micro-payments between Coinbase accounts.
