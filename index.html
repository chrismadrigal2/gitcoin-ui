What is Gitcoin?
Gitcoin is a way for people to easily give one-time or weekly donations to their favorite projects. Think of it as social philanthropy using bitcoin. We are currently built on top of the Coinbase API which means that in order to receive donation with Gitcoin you'll need to have a Coinbase account.
